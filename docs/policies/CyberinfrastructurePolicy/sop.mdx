These standard operating procedures govern the processes of administering CIROH Cyberinfrastructure's cloud and on-premise resources.

For more accessible guidance on requesting access to computing resources, see the [Infrastructure Access](/docs/services/access) page.

> *If you have any questions about these standard operating procedures, please reach out to us at [ciroh-it-admin@ua.edu](mailto:ciroh-it-admin@ua.edu).*
